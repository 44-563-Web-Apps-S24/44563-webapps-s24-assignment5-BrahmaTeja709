Hotchocolate

Hot chocolate is made by melting, shaving, or grating dark, white, or milk chocolate and combining it with either water or milk. The intensity of the drink can be customized based on your preference, with milk resulting in a richer flavor compared to using water alone.

Hotchocolate Wikipedia Link
Neera

Neera, also known as palm nectar, is the sap extracted from the inflorescence of different toddy palm species, serving as a nutritious health beverage. Referred to as sweet toddy, Neera does not contain any alcohol and is recognized as padaneer in Tamil Nadu.

Neera Wikipedia Link
